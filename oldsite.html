About Us

Organic

We are committed to growing without the use of pesticides, herbicides and inorganic fertilizers. We use crop rotation, green manure, compost and small amounts of bone meal and chicken manure to create a rich and healthy soil environment. 

Local

We are located on the outskirts of Davis, California. All the produce we sell are grown right here. Our members can choose to pick up their weekly basket at the farm or have it delivered to their doorstep.

Seasonal

We grow a variety of vegetables, berries and fruits. The selection varies with the season, which is how food should be enjoyed. A spring basket may include kale, chard, lettuce, carrots, asparagus, artichokes, sweet peas, fennel,  and berries, among other things. A summer basket may include tomatoes, zucchini, eggplant, melons, basil, peppers, cucumbers, peaches etc. A fall basket may include broccoli, cabbage, pumpkins, winter squash, green beans and watermelons.
